Metagame & Matchups

Understanding the local metagame and knowing how to handle various matchups is crucial for success. Here are a few tips and strategies against some of the popular decks you might face in your local scene.

Matchup 1: Aggro Decks

Aggro decks tend to pressure us early, but we can usually stabilize with some of our removal spells and control elements. Key cards to watch for:

  • Removal spells like "Cast Down" and "Snuff Out"
  • Sideboard options like "Duress" to strip away early threats

Matchup 2: Control Decks

Against control, we need to be patient and try to play around counterspells. Consider using discard spells to remove their key answers.

  • "Duress" to strip away counterspells or removal
  • Keep threats spread out to avoid a full board wipe

Matchup 3: Combo Decks

Combo decks rely on executing a specific combo to win. Our strategy should be disrupting their combo setup while maintaining board control.

  • Sideboard cards like "Deadly Dispute" for card advantage
  • Use targeted removal to prevent key pieces from hitting the battlefield

Metagame Tips

Here are some general tips for success in the current metagame:

  • Adapt your sideboard depending on the most popular decks at your locals.
  • Make sure your deck is well-rounded with enough removal and card advantage tools.
  • Know your threats and learn to maximize your plays during the midgame when you can gain an advantage.
