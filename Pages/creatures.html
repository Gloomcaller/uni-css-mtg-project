Avenging Hunter

Aside from being a 5/4 trampler Avenging Hunter gives us great bit of advantage by taking us to the Undercity dungeon by giving us initiative. You can see the dungeon by hovering over the card.

Crypt Rats

Ctypt Rats are used for borad wipes and also for delivering a final kamikaze in the late game to finish our oponent.

Thorn of the Black Rose

We use Thorn primarly to give us The Monarch so we can have even more card draw. Sometimes its risky to play since our oponent could just take The Monarch so it is important to time this card well. Also it's a 1/3 with deathtouch so we can threaten big creatures with it.

Troll of Khazad-dum

Troll is another large creature with 6/5 3+ menace, but it also has swampcycling so it's not a dead card in hand for the early game.

Vampire Sovereign

Sovereign is a bigger threat than people realise since it steals 3 life and alows us to create life advantage so that we can use Crypt Rats later on. Also before all that it is a 3/4 flyer that can dish out some damage.
