Bojuka Bog

While it is a bit slow we do have enough card draw to include graveyard hate in the main deck. This is a meta call that depends on your locals meta.

Golgari Rot Farm

Simple bounce land that we can use with Bojuka Bog, Khalni Garden or Witch's Cottage depending on our current situation and needs.

Swamp

Regarding basic lands we only run swamps, but you could get away with one or two Forests.

Khalni Garden

Gardens are a precious blocking resource that generates 0/1 Plants which we can use for blocking, targets and a bunch of other things. Simply put a great card.

Witch's Cottage

Cottace is used to return a creature card from out graveyard if we need it, and is just a swamp in the late game.
