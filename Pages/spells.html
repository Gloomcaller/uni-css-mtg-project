Cast Down

Simple instant removal that comes in handy when needed.

Deadly Dispute

Dispute is the king of card advantage in this deck. It lets us draw 2 cards while giving us one Treasure that we can use in synergy with pretty much anything. Hover to see the Treasure.

Duress

Duress provides us with a unique type of advantage that lets us see exactly what our oponent is holding thus creating an invaluable knowlage as soon as we play it. Also knocking one card from the oponents hand is a very nice bonus.

Hopeless Nightmare

Nightmare does pretty much everything our deck does in small amounts, it knock a card from hand, deals 2 damage, lets us scry 2 and gives us a target while being a one mana drop.

Snuff Out

Snuff out lets us remove a non black creature while we have no mana so it's a great card for when we want to bluff. Since we are generating life advantage by the nature of the deck this card is pretty much always free to cast.

Spinning Darkness

Another amazing card that created a life advantage for free. We can use the 3 damage to remove almost anything.
